About Crystal Chemical Products

Crystal Chemical Products was founded in 1997 as a small-scale industry by the Late Director, Mr. Sam Mutebi, specializing in the manufacture of cleaning detergents under the Makula Brand.

Located along Salaama Road, Plot No. 86, Block 263, Luwafu, Nakinyuguzi, Makindye, Kampala, Uganda, Crystal Chemical Products has grown into a trusted local manufacturer known for quality, consistency, and reliability.

Crystal products are developed to high quality standards using a combination of product science, technology, and carefully selected ingredients to produce cleaning solutions that are effective, dependable, and suitable for both home and commercial use.

We are committed to delivering products that meet the everyday hygiene and cleaning needs of our customers. We also stand behind the quality of our products with a customer guarantee: if any product is found to be defective or below the expected standard, it may be returned to Crystal for exchange.
