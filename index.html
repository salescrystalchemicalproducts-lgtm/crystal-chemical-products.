About us

Built on local manufacturing, quality and dependable service.

Crystal Chemical Products was founded in 1997 as a small-scale industry by the Late Director, Mr. Sam Mutebi, manufacturing cleaning detergents under the trusted Makula Brand.

We serve homes, schools, hotels, offices, supermarkets, health facilities, retail shops and distributors with products designed for everyday cleaning and professional hygiene needs.

Our goal is to become Uganda’s most trusted household and industrial cleaning products brand through consistent quality, affordability and reliable customer support.
