About Crystal Chemical Products

Crystal Chemical Products was founded in 1997 as a small-scale industry by the Late Director, Mr. Sam Mutebi, specializing in manufacturing cleaning detergents under the Makula Brand.

We are located along Salaama Road, Plot No. 86, Block 263, Luwafu, Nakinyuguzi, Makindye, Kampala, Uganda.

Makula Cleaning products are among the best products available locally. We are dedicated to producing the best products on the market today. Each product is developed to high quality standards.

We combine the latest product science and technology with carefully selected ingredients to produce products that are superior in quality, dependable in performance, and suitable for both home and commercial use.

Crystal Chemical Products offers customer guarantee no questions asked.
